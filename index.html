About

QUASAR Lab India is a research collective focused on foundational and applied work in quantum information science, machine learning, systems, and robotics. We collaborate with academia and industry to build open tools, publish impactful work, and mentor the next generation of researchers and engineers.

Research Areas

Quantum Computing

Algorithms, error mitigation, simulation, and applications.

Artificial Intelligence

Foundation models, agents, reasoning, safety, and alignment.

Systems & Tooling

Distributed systems, compilers, performance engineering, and infra.

Robotics & Autonomy

Perception, control, multi-agent coordination, and HRI.
